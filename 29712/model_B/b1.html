What is Governance?

Governance refers to the process of decision-making and the process by which decisions are implemented (or not implemented). It involves formal and informal actors, structures, and processes.

Understanding Politics

Politics involves the activities associated with the governance of a country or area, especially the debate or conflict among individuals or parties having or hoping to achieve power.

Differences Between Governance and Politics

Governance is about the rules and processes for decision-making, while politics often deals with power struggles and policy-making.
